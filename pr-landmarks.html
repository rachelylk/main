Visual Landmarks

Landmark detection is relevant for many medical imaging analyses. As such, we developed a method for obtaining landmark predictions that includes uncertainty of the predictions. This method includes network calibration and a novel statistic called ‘Expected Radial Error’ to obtain uncertainty. This method allows for output certainty heatmaps for better landmark localization.
