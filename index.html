Projects

My group's OxMedIS software for Medical Image Segmentation is capable of processing MRI, CT, microCT, ultrasound and other medical and biological data. We have developed algorithms to partition each 3D image into regions with similar properties. These images are then analysed further so that particular features (such as bones, organs or blood vessels) can be identified within them and segmented out. Once labelled, the features can be reconstructed as 3D shapes and displayed or 3D printed on our Makerbot 2X.
